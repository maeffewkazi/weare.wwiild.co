We are looking for a full-stack Laravel web developer to join our amazing team.

About Thursday

We are a successful and award winning design studio based in Winchester.

Opportunity/Role

This is an exciting new opportunity to join our successful in-house development team, working for high-profile clients such as Samsung and Estée Lauder.

You will be working on projects ranging from new and existing websites through to cutting-edge web apps using the latest technologies, such as augmented reality, NFC and web sockets.

Requirements

You will need a keen eye for detail and design.
As an agency with a strong emphasis on creativity, we build beautiful brands and visual identities. And so, as a development team, we need to ensure these brands are beautifully delivered right through to the final, live execution.

We are looking for candidates who are excited about the web and about innovating.

You will work closely with the rest of our team, collaborating with other developers as well as our designers, digital marketing team and project managers. You will be expected to liaise directly with our clients too.

As our projects need to picked up and worked on by other members of our team, we will expect you to adopt a clear and consistent coding style.

Key skills

• HTML

• Javascript
• Jquery
• SASS/LESS
• NPM/Webpack/Gulp/Grunt
• PHP
• MYSQL
• Git

Optional skills

• Vuejs
• Wordpress
• Table tennis and tea making

Starting ASAP, with a competitive salary
