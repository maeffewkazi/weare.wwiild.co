It may look like 70’s postmodernism in gingerbread form, but inside Cromwell House there’s a minimalist’s dream-come-true waiting for you. The top floors, flooded with light and overlooking the hills, moors and rooftops of Winchester,

Custom-designed for our needs, this open-plan expanse encompasses meeting areas, relaxation spaces and extensive hot-desking. Upstairs are a sunlit workroom and roof terrace, giving plenty of options to us laptop nomads: a relaxed working style that works for us. Moving around encourages the ideas to arrive, and with no hierarchy or closed doors, Thursday is as open as it looks.

And it does look minimal. Our monochrome brand palette is set off with the neutral tones of natural finishes: exposed concrete, clean wood. Like our insight-led approach, we like to get back to the beginnings of things, finding the truth and lighting it beautifully. It is a space that feels both calm and alive with possibilities.
